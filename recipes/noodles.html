Jjajjangmyeon (Korean Noodles)

A korean staple. These noodles are unique and many haven't tried it outside of Korea. Give this dish a try to get a taste of something truly special!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of potato
  • Noodles of your choice
  • 1 cup of chopped pork
  • 1 tbs of black bean paste

Directions

  1. Boil the noodles while frying up the pork alongside onions
  2. Once noodles are done, take out the water and add in the black bean paste
  3. Add the onions and pork to the noodles and mix it all well til it looks like the image above
