PIZZA

Pizza is the best food in the world. Add some potatoes and cheese and you can have the world at your feet. There is nothing you cannot accomplish.

What more is there to say? Make this recipe and don't let your dreams be dreams.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Potatoes
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Make the flour into a dough
  2. Flatten the dough and add Tomato Sauce
  3. Add Cheese and Potatoes
  4. Place in oven and burn it for 15 minutes
  5. Take it out and enjoy with tabasco
